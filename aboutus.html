Our Process


Aromatherapy is based around the use of essential oils which show great therapeutic benefit. Aromatherapy can help in relation to healing, where the use of natural based plant products, which are more commonly known as essential oils, assist in the healing process both physically and psychologically.

The way in which aromatherapy works is mainly through inhalation, however some other processes such as ingestion can be used too. In relation to the FLOW Web Store, candles can be of great use when it comes to the aromatherapeutic benefits. It works in comparison to lavender for example, where inhaling it can be a way in which someone can reduce their anxiety. Aromatherapy is often used as an alternative to other forms of treatments, such as massages.

Benefits of Aromatherapy Many benefits arise through the use of aroma candles, for example: - Enhancement of your mood - Relief of Stress - Pain Relief - Improvement of Air Quality in surrounding areas.
